Who We Are

Founded in Hong Kong, Buir Lake Laboratories sits at the dynamic intersection of scientific research and academic publishing. Unlike traditional publishers, we are born from the laboratory.

Our mission is twofold: to push the boundaries of optical imaging technologies and to build a more transparent, efficient, and accessible platform for scientific communication.

We believe that the future of biology lies in the precision of light. By integrating cutting-edge research in ultrafast optics with a rigorous open-access publishing model, we aim to accelerate discoveries that illuminate the mechanisms of life.

Core Focus Areas

Academic Publishing

We are the proud publisher of Optical Biology (hosted at biology.ac), a peer-reviewed open-access journal focused on Ultrafast Spectroscopy, Non-linear Optics, and AI-driven Analysis.

Optical Biology (biology.ac)

Spectroscopic Microscopy

Pioneering advanced label-free imaging techniques, including Stimulated Raman Scattering (SRS) and Coherent Anti-Stokes Raman Scattering (CARS) microscopy, for high-speed, chemically specific biological visualization.

Nonlinear Optics

Our latest product line includes high-performance Ti:Sapphire & Yb-doped OPAs, broadly tunable femtosecond sources, and precision components like low-dispersion mirrors and pulse-shaping etalons.
